# Privacy Policy for ScanStock

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to ScanStock ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our inventory management application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use ScanStock.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using ScanStock, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use ScanStock, you may provide us with the following types of information:

- **Product Information**: Names, descriptions, barcodes, prices, and stock quantities of products you add to your inventory
- **Product Photos**: Images you take or upload for your products
- **Purchase Information**: If you upgrade to Pro, your purchase is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly.

### Information Collected Automatically

ScanStock is designed with privacy in mind. We do **NOT** automatically collect:

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Advertising identifiers

### Camera and Photo Access

- **Camera**: Used only when you actively choose to scan barcodes or take product photos. The camera is not accessed in the background.
- **Photo Library**: Used only when you choose to add a photo from your gallery. We do not access photos without your explicit action.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is used solely to:

- Display your inventory within the App
- Enable barcode scanning functionality
- Generate QR codes for your products
- Create backup files (with Pro)
- Export reports in CSV or PDF format (with Pro)

We do **NOT** use your information for:

- Advertising or marketing
- Analytics or tracking
- Sharing with third parties
- Creating user profiles

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All your inventory data, including product information and photos, is stored **locally on your device**. This means:

- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or backup
- We cannot access your inventory data
- Your data is not stored on our servers
- If you delete the App without creating a backup, your data will be permanently lost

### Backup Files

When you create a backup (Pro feature):

- The backup file is created on your device
- You choose where to save or share the file
- We do not have access to your backup files
- Backup files contain all your product data and photos

### Security Measures

While we do not store your data on external servers, we recommend:

- Keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock
- Creating regular backups of your inventory
- Storing backup files in a secure location

---

## Third-Party Services

### App Store Purchases

If you purchase Pro features:

- **Apple App Store**: Purchases on iOS are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.
- **Google Play Store**: Purchases on Android are processed by Google. Google's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

We receive only a confirmation of your purchase status, not your payment details.

### No Third-Party Analytics

ScanStock does **NOT** integrate with:

- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Crash reporting services

---

## Children's Privacy

ScanStock is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any personal information, the App can be safely used by users of all ages under appropriate supervision.

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all your data within the App at any time
- **Modify**: Edit or delete any product information
- **Export**: Export your data to CSV or PDF (Pro)
- **Backup**: Create complete backups of your data (Pro)
- **Delete**: Remove all data by deleting the App or clearing App data

### Permissions

You can manage App permissions through your device settings:

- **Camera**: Required for barcode scanning and taking photos. Can be disabled if not needed.
- **Photo Library**: Required only if you want to add photos from your gallery. Can be disabled if not needed.

---

## Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally:

- Data remains on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the App removes all associated data
- We do not retain any copies of your data

---

## International Users

ScanStock can be used internationally. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, there are no cross-border data transfers to consider. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location.
