## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General

**Q: Is ScanStock free to use?**
A: Yes! ScanStock is free to download and use. The free version includes unlimited products, barcode scanning, stock tracking, and search. Pro features like backup and export require a one-time purchase.

**Q: Does ScanStock work offline?**
A: Yes! ScanStock works completely offline. All your data is stored on your device. You don't need an internet connection to manage your inventory.

**Q: What languages does ScanStock support?**
A: ScanStock currently supports English and Spanish. More languages coming soon!

**Q: Can I use ScanStock on multiple devices?**
A: With the free version, your data stays on one device. With Pro Cloud (coming soon), you'll be able to sync across multiple devices.

### Products & Scanning

**Q: What types of barcodes can ScanStock scan?**
A: ScanStock can scan most common barcode formats including UPC-A, UPC-E, EAN-8, EAN-13, Code 128, Code 39, and QR codes.

**Q: Can I add products without a barcode?**
A: Yes! You can add products manually by entering the product name, price, and stock quantity. Barcodes are optional.

**Q: Can I add photos to products?**
A: Yes! You can take a photo with your camera or choose one from your gallery for each product.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many products I can add?**
A: No! You can add unlimited products with the free version.

### Data & Backup

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All your inventory data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any of your data on external servers.

**Q: How do I backup my inventory?**
A: With Pro, you can create a complete backup (ZIP file) that includes all your products and photos. Go to Settings > Backup & Export to create a backup.

**Q: Can I export my inventory to Excel?**
A: Yes! With Pro, you can export your inventory to CSV format, which is compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, and other spreadsheet apps.

**Q: What happens if I delete the app?**
A: If you delete the app without creating a backup, your data will be lost. We recommend creating regular backups with the Pro version.

### Pro Features

**Q: What's included in Pro Local?**
A: Pro Local is a one-time purchase that includes:
- Complete backup (ZIP with photos)
- Export to CSV (Excel compatible)
- Professional PDF reports
- Restore from backup

**Q: What's included in Pro Cloud?**
A: Pro Cloud is a subscription that includes everything in Pro Local, plus:
- Cloud sync across devices
- Automatic cloud backups
- Priority support

**Q: Can I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Backup & Export and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Pro purchase.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The barcode scanner isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Make sure you've granted camera permission to ScanStock. Try the following:
1. Go to your device Settings
2. Find ScanStock in the app list
3. Enable Camera permission
4. Also try better lighting or cleaning the barcode

**Q: My products disappeared. What happened?**
A: If your products disappeared, it may be due to:
- App data being cleared
- App being reinstalled
- Device reset

This is why we recommend creating regular backups with Pro.

**Q: The app is running slowly. What can I do?**
A: Try the following:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Make sure you have enough storage space
3. Restart your device
